# Ascend Competitive And Future Roadmap

## Positioning

Ascend should remain the development, education, incubation, content, and readiness platform. Emerge Globally should remain the professional representation and management tier.

## Phase 2 Foundations

- Category-specific application requirements.
- Admin-manageable category and opportunity metadata.
- Applicant media review queue with signed private media URLs.
- Confirmation number and applicant status communications.
- Training/development program intake paths.
- Creator, production, and content-studio opportunity matching.
- Analytics for category demand, review status, media completeness, and source attribution.

## Required Action

Treat Phase 2 as a product design and database modeling effort, not a quick dropdown expansion. The next implementation should introduce a normalized category/role/media-requirement layer before adding many more public fields.
